\section*{Objective}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Objective}

\begin{itemize}
    \item To understand the principle of the Superposition Theorem and its application in linear DC circuits.
    \item To experimentally verify that the response in any element is the sum of individual effects of each independent source.
    \item To learn how to correctly turn off independent voltage and current sources.
    \item To compare measured and theoretical values to confirm the validity of the theorem.
    \item To gain practical skills in building DC circuits and using measuring instruments.
\end{itemize}
